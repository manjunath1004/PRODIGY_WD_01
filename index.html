Project

Healthcare
Healthcare is an online platform designed to connect patients with doctors for urgent medical consultations. The platform enables patients to seek advice from healthcare experts through live video calls, especially in non-physical emergencies or situations where immediate treatment suggestions are critical.

Purpose/Goal: To address emergency medical issues by providing expert advice and temporary treatment recommendations that can stabilize the patient until they are admitted to a hospital.

Unique Features/Challenges Solved

  • Real-time video call functionality to connect patients with specialized doctors.
  • Seamless user interface for quick access to medical help.
  • Advanced AI integration for pre-diagnosis and directing patients to the right expert.

Technologies Used:
  • Frontend: HTML, Tailwind CSS, JavaScript, React.js
  • Backend: Node.js, Python
  • Database: MongoDB
  • Other Tools: AI integration for pre-diagnosis

Type of Project: Team Project: A collaborative effort built with a team of developers and designers.
